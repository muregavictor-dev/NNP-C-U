Who We Are

Nyeri National Polytechnic Christian Union is a vibrant community focused on spiritual growth, fellowship, and service. We meet regularly for worship, Bible study, and outreach activities to strengthen our faith and impact our community positively.

Vision

To be a radiant light of hope and faith, influencing every sphere of the polytechnic.

Mission

To equip students to grow in faith, lead impactful lives, and serve the community.
